about me

Hello! My name is Piotr, I was born and raised in Gdansk, Poland. I am a husband and father of one. Graduated in the field of Economics and Public Administration, trying to find myself a new place in IT industry. For my whole life I was looking for a job which will bring me a lot of fun, excitement and satisfaction. And I have recently found out that its all about programming ;)

In regards of my hobby, I am very interested in number of things, just to mention a few of them:

Most important in my life and most loved - my daughter and wife.

Another thing which is very important for me is the travelling. So far I have visited 22 countries, mostly in Europe. It's very vaulable for me to learn new things and different approach from different cultures. Seeing new places and meeting new people brings a lot of new experiences. On my 'to go' list there are United States of America, Cuba and Mexico.

Football is my favorite sport, I am a fan of Polish Football League, which might be not so common, due to the crappy teams in Poland. My most loved team is from my mother city Gdansk - Lechia, which I am supporting since I remember :)

I am a great fan of all thriller and crime movies! Especially Korean movies, they are doing best thrillers on the earth!

Last, but not least. I have fallen in love with riding a bicycle for a second time in my life 1 year ago, when I have started to have some issues with my knees, and that was the only activity I could do. Long trips on the bicycle? That's when the fun starts!
